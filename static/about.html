Our Mission

Free. Accessible. Fun.

We believe that high-quality education should be a right, not a privilege. Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional, expensive tuition classes and the students who need them most.

By connecting passionate university students with younger pupils, we create a cycle of learning and mentorship. We aim to lift the financial burden off parents' shoulders, ensuring that no child is left behind due to cost.

Dracarys is more than just a platform; it's a movement to facilitate child growth and make learning an enjoyable, community-driven experience.
