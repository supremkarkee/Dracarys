About Dracarys

We believe every child deserves access to an inspiring, affordable, and safe learning experience — no matter their background or budget.

Our Mission

Dracarys is a community-driven tuition marketplace built by university students, for school students. We connect enthusiastic university students and subject experts with primary and secondary school pupils, bridging the gap between academic levels through peer-to-peer learning. Our platform makes education more accessible, more affordable, and genuinely more fun.

Our Core Values

🎯

Accessibility

Quality education should not be limited by financial barriers. We offer affordable community-based tutoring for all families.

🛡️

Child Safety

Every tutee is a minor. We verify all tutors, provide parental oversight tools, and monitor all communications for safety.

🌟

Engagement

Learning should be exciting. Our tutors use interactive methods, games, and real-life examples to make subjects come alive.

🤝

Community

We believe in sharing knowledge cooperatively, for mutual benefit. Dracarys is built on the principle of giving back.

⚖️

Fairness

Transparent pricing, fair treatment of tutors, and equal access regardless of background, gender, or learning needs.

📈

Growth

We track and celebrate progress. Every session note, every review, and every badge represents a step forward.

🛡️ Our Commitment to Safety & Ethics

Dracarys was designed with child safeguarding at its core. All tutors are verified with university email addresses. Parents have full visibility over all messaging between tutors and their children. We comply fully with GDPR — collecting only the minimum data required. Our platform includes a content flagging system, moderation controls, and a clear code of conduct for all tutors. We are committed to inclusivity, accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA), and bias-free matching.
